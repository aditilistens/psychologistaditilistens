About Me

Hi, I'm Aditi Trivedi, a psychologist with a Master's degree in Psychology. I specialize in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Rational Emotive Behavioral Therapy (REBT), Solution-Focused Brief Therapy (SFBT), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), and mindfulness practices. My areas of expertise include helping individuals with depression, anxiety, eating disorders, low self-esteem, and body image issues.
